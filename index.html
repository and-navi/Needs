Needs

This is an initiative taken in order to help the needy person/community/area who are financially not sound enough to fulfill their basic needs and so thus the idea of Central Reserved Fund has been emerged out. This page will work as the center for all. Target is to link maximum people centrally so that they can make a donation for a noble cause. All you have to do is to donate a very little amount of Rs.10 a month and furthermore ask your known ones for the same. With this little contribution of yours, which you easily spend on daily basis on other things, will add to a very significant sum which will definitely bring a smile on many faces, which includes you. That noble act will, for sure, make a difference. Then you will be proud of yourself for such a spiritual work.
